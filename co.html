About The College

The college seeks effectively to provide all departments of the governorate that suffer from a shortage of computer science and mathematics specializations and to provide training and development activities for the community and various industrial, service and industrial facilities in the governorate. The college has a large, distinguished and important role in enhancing the scientific and cultural aspect of the university through the scientific seminars it holds intensively. Striving in the future to open new scientific departments and branches to keep pace with the rapid scientific developments in the field of its scientific specialization. Striving in the future to open a consulting office to provide the governorate and the university with scientific consultations and proposals to develop the work of government institutions according to modern scientific methods in the field of information technology and e-governance.

Education

2007-2008

Computer Science And Mathmatic

The College of Computer Science and Mathematics / Tikrit University was established in 2007, and received its first batch of students in the academic year 2007-2008. The college started with the departments of Computer Science and Mathematics, which split from the College of Science. Thus, the college has four study stages for the academic year 2007-2008. The teaching staff in the college is (55) lecturers in various specializations, professors, assistant professors, lecturers, and assistant lecturers, and the administrative staff is about (51) technical and administrative employees.

2024-2025

Cybersecurity Science

The Department of Cybersecurity Sciences was established in the College of Computer Science and Mathematics in 2025.

Services

College Magazine

Tikrit Journal for Computer Sciences and Mathematics is a quarterly journal concerned with scientific and academic research issued by the College of Computer Science and Mathematics, and published by Tikrit University/Salah AL Deen / Iraq. It is concerned with global events and issues, specializing in all aspects of Computer science And Mathematics Science.

Academic promotions

About the Committee's Work The Scientific Promotions Committee at the college promotes the scientific promotion transactions for faculty members after they have fulfilled the required conditions, relying on the Scientific Promotions Instructions No. 36 of 1992 and subsequent amendments and Instructions No. (167) of 2017.

Electronic Extraction

Electronic plagiarism program Our university - College of Computer Science and Mathematics provides an electronic plagiarism examination service (Turnitin) for research, scientific papers, master's theses and doctoral dissertations for faculty members and researchers from within. A receipt for the plagiarism amount is cut off at the financial department in the university presidency.
